My Manifesto

Hi voter, I am Yulia, 4th year Computing student who loves Imperial and wants to make it an even better place!

I have been volunteering for the union through out my degree. I have been a year rep for JMC and Computing, represented engineering students at union council. I have been actively involved with my departamental society (DoCSoc) first as events officer, significantly increasing the number of events, students participation and engagement with the society, then as a chair doubling the societies sponsorship and further increasing the students' involvement with the society. In my free time I have been a member of the university cheerleading team and taught kids to program.

I absolutely loved to be able to improve Computing students' time here at Imperial and I would really like to dedicate a year of my life to making your university experience even better.

What I want to improve:

Welfare. I want this year to focus on welfare and especially stress and depression problems at our university more than any other year before it. I want to achieve better welfare representation across all departments and work together with the College to improve the personal tutor support students are getting. I believe that every week should be a stress relief week and you don't need to have signed up for the right mailing list to hear about all the welfare events going on around campus.

Innovation. More recogniton from the university for the side projects, I will campaign for the university to give more recognition for the extracutticular projects students are dedicating a large proportion of their time to. I also want to improve the engagement between the departments to give more students the opportunity to work on cross-departamental projects as I think this way we will see even more innovative products being produced by Imperial College students.

A union that works for you. Easier way to handle clubs and societies transactions! Having a technology background myself, I believe the e-activities should be far easier to use, we should have an online payment option for sponsors and donors. We should have a better way to see what events are going on campus by utilising facebook feeds. I would happily volunteer my Computing skills and put extra hours towards making the union technology even better. I would love to attract more students to volunteer their programming skills to improve union's technology as it could be a great opportunity for students who are looking for a side project but don't have an idea of what to build.

Equal representation. Increase the representation of postgraduate and business school students at the union. I feel like this category of students especially those that are here only for a year don't get the best out of their time here ..

Most importantly I will be open to hear your critisism and ideas on how to make the union the better place. I believe that being a president of the union is not an individual job but it is about collaborating with students, listening to their suggestions and feedback. I want to work together with the students of this university to make this place ideal for their needs.

I'd love to hear from you

I'm eager to hear your thoughts on what we can do to improve our university experience! Send me an email at yulia.bulgakova12@imperial.ac.uk
